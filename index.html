Selected Publications

On the existence of solutions for a class of non-linear Schrödinger equations

[1] J. Doe, A. Smith, B. Chen. "On the existence of solutions for a class of non-linear Schrödinger equations," Journal of Mathematical Physics, Vol. 65, No. 3, pp. 033501, 2024.

Abstract: We investigate the existence and uniqueness of solutions for the non-linear Schrödinger equation with a potential term given by: $$-i \frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = -\frac{1}{2m} \nabla^2 u + V(x)u + \beta |u|^2 u$$ where $u(x,t)$ is the complex-valued wave function, $V(x)$ is the potential, and $\beta$ is a real constant. Our approach uses variational methods in Sobolev spaces.

[PDF]

Numerical stability of finite difference schemes for parabolic PDEs

[2] J. Doe, L. Garcia. "Numerical stability of finite difference schemes for parabolic PDEs," SIAM Journal on Numerical Analysis, Vol. 60, No. 5, pp. 2800-2820, 2023.

Abstract: This paper analyzes the numerical stability of several explicit and implicit finite difference schemes for solving the one-dimensional heat equation: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}$$ We demonstrate that stability conditions are governed by the Courant-Friedrichs-Lewy (CFL) condition for explicit methods, specifically $ \alpha \frac{\Delta t}{(\Delta x)^2} \le \frac{1}{2} $.
